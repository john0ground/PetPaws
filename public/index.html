Meet The Team

Dr. Maya Rodriguez

VETERINARY PRACTICE MANAGER

I feel incredibly fortunate to do what I love every day. Being surrounded by adorable pets and dedicated staff members makes coming to work an absolute joy. Ensuring the well-being of our furry patients brings me immense satisfaction, and seeing tails wag and hearing purrs of contentment reaffirms why I chose this profession.

Our team is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of veterinary care, utilizing advanced equipment to diagnose and treat our furry patients efficiently. We believe that providing a sterile and well-equipped environment not only ensures the safety of the animals but also reflects our commitment to excellence in veterinary medicine.
